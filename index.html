How to Play

  • You crashland on a mysterious planet and must repair your ship. Repair your ship with the help of Pikmin!

  • These are Pikmin! They'll be your trusty helpers to help you get out of The Forest of Hope. Use them to gather tokens, fight enemies, and retrieve the ship part you need to escape the planet.
    This is a Pikmin Spawner. Use it to create more Pikmin! Gather spawn tokens and defeat enemies so you can spawn more Pikmin.
    This is a spawn token. Use Pikmin to retrieve these to increase how many Pikmin you can grow!
    This is an enemy. You'll need to take care of them before you can retrieve the ship part. Retrieve defeated enemies and bring them back to the Pikmin Spawner!
    This is a ship part. Retrieve this with Pikmin and return it to your ship to repair it!
